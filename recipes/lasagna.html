Lasagna Recipe

Description

This homemade lasagna recipe is delicious and easy to make.

It features layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese with a golden cheesy topping

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Mozzarella
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until al dente.
  2. Brown beef & sausage with garlic.
  3. Add sauce and spices; simmer.
  4. Mix ricotta with egg and parsley.
  5. Layer: sauce → pasta → ricotta mix → mozzarella.
  6. Repeat layers until full; finish with mozzarella.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes..
  8. Rest 10 minutes, slice, serve.
