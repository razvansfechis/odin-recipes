Beef Burger

Description

Who doesn't love burgers?

Here I've prepared for you a quick and easy recipe to make a delicious beef burger

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80/20)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo

Steps

  1. Season beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Form patties slightly larger than the buns.
  3. Cook patties on a hot pan or grill, 3-4 min per side.
  4. Add cheese in the last minute to melt.
  5. Toast buns lightly.
  6. Assemble: bun → patty → toppings → sauces.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
