Pizza Margherita Recipe

Description

This is one of the simpler recipes, it has very few ingredients

Fun fact: It was named after Queen Margherita after visiting Naples

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • San Marzano tomatoes
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil
  • A drizzle of extra virgin olive oil
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll dough into a thin round.
  3. Spread sauce lightly over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella slices or chunks.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.
  6. Top with basil leaves.
  7. Drizzle olive oil, slice, and serve.
